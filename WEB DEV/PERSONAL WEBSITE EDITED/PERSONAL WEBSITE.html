The mind behind it all.

I am Jasper R. Turado, a third-year student, a thinker, a fighter, but most importantly, a cat-lover. I live in Valenzuela City, where I spent my days studying from preparatory to high school. Growing up in this vibrant city has shaped my perspective on life and instilled in me a strong sense of community. From exploring its bustling streets to immersing myself in its rich culture, Valenzuela City holds a special place in my heart. As a 19-year-old student, I am passionate about delving into the realms of knowledge, constantly seeking to expand my understanding of the world around me. Whether it's through academic pursuits or personal experiences, I thrive on the opportunity to learn and grow. That is why I made a decision to step out of my comfort zone and wander the streets of Manila, study in a well-renowned school known as the Polytechnic University of the Philippines. Currently, I am taking a Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, and I am expected to graduate in 2025.

To know more about me, do not hesitate to navigate this small website that I made for myself. If you have inquiries, contact me at jasperturado29@gmail.com or you can reach out to me via my social media accounts under the same name.

The math I do.

Math has always been my thing. Having participated in multiple contests ever since I was young, I’m pretty confident that I’ve built a really good foundation over this subject. Algebra and geometry are where I excel the most, finding joy in unraveling complex equations and geometric proofs. But my love for math extends beyond the confines of the classroom. As a programmer, I find myself applying mathematical principles in my daily life, whether it's optimizing code efficiency or problem-solving in algorithm development. Math isn't just a subject to me; it's a tool that empowers me to tackle challenges creatively and analytically. From designing algorithms to analyzing data trends, my proficiency in mathematics enriches every aspect of my life and fuels my passion for continuous learning and exploration.

I've been a consistent placer in MMC during elementary and high school, and an outstanding awardee in Mathematics. In fact, I’ve recently won in many mathematical contests hosted by the top schools in the Philippines including University of Santo Tomas and Ateneo de Manila University. I’ve also ventured in national and international contests hosted by UP Diliman and other international organizations but wasn’t fortunate enough to bag any titles. The most recent achievement in Math that I can proudly brag to anyone is becoming a part of Photomath Marketplace’s Team of Math Experts.

With each discovery, I am propelled further into the depths of this ever-evolving field, eager to uncover new methodologies and applications that will shape the future of technology and society.

The music I listen to.

Music holds a special place in my heart, serving as a soundtrack to my emotions and a source of solace during moments of boredom. To me, music is one of the most incredible inventions ever, with its ability to evoke feelings and transport us to different worlds. My taste in music spans across genres and cultures, reflecting my eclectic musical palate. The games I play.

Gaming is more than just a hobby for me; it's a journey filled with excitement, camaraderie, and endless adventures. Among the myriad of games I've explored, Genshin Impact stands out as my absolute favorite, transporting me to a breathtaking world of wonder and magic. Whether embarking on quests, uncovering hidden treasures, or mastering elemental combat, Genshin captivates me with its immersive gameplay and captivating storyline. For casual fun with friends, Mobile Legends offers the perfect escape, where we team up to battle it out in thrilling matches, laughing and strategizing together along the way. In the realm of nostalgia, Ragnarok holds a special place in my heart, evoking memories of epic battles and unforgettable quests. Meanwhile, the addictive gameplay of Supercell's titles like Clash Royale and Clash of Clans keeps me hooked for hours on end, as I build, strategize, and clash my way to victory. And amidst the vast landscapes of creativity, Minecraft provides a canvas for endless exploration and boundless imagination. However, amidst the joy and excitement of gaming, there's one game I despise—the game called life, with its unpredictable challenges and unforgiving rules. But within the digital realms of gaming, I find solace, inspiration, and a sense of belonging, making each gaming session a cherished escape from the complexities of reality.
