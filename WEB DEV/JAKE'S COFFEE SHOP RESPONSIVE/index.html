J O B S
CURRENT JOB
POSTINGS

To apply for a job at our coffee shop, please submit your resume and cover letter to jake@jcoffee.com or drop them off in person at our physical store at Cornelia Street. Be sure to indicate the position you are applying for in the subject line or on your application. Read each position below to know their description.
BARISTA
-Prepare and serve coffee beverages according to established recipes and standards.
-Provide exceptional customer service by greeting and assisting customers, taking orders, and answering questions.
-Maintain cleanliness and organization of the coffee bar and work areas.
-Operate coffee-making equipment safely and efficiently.
-Previous barista experience preferred but not required.
KITCHEN STAFF
- Assist with food preparation, including chopping vegetables, preparing sauces, and assembling dishes.
- Operate kitchen equipment safely and efficiently, following all food safety and sanitation guidelines.
- Maintain cleanliness and organization in the kitchen, including washing dishes and sanitizing surfaces.
- Communicate effectively with the rest of the kitchen team to ensure smooth operation during busy periods.
- Previous kitchen experience preferred but not required.
ASSISTANT MANAGER
- Assist with daily operations, including opening and closing procedures, inventory management, and staff supervision.
- Provide leadership and support to the team, including training and development of staff members.
- Ensure compliance with company policies and procedures, as well as health and safety regulations.
- Handle customer inquiries and resolve any issues or complaints in a professional manner.
- Previous supervisory or management experience in the hospitality industry preferred.
SERVER
- Greet customers, take orders, and deliver food and beverages in a timely manner.
- Provide attentive and friendly service to ensure a positive customer experience.
- Assist with cleaning and resetting tables, as well as maintaining cleanliness in the dining area.
- Collaborate with kitchen staff to ensure orders are accurate and delivered promptly.
- Previous restaurant or customer service experience preferred.
